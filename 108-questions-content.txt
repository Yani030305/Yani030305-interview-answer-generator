108 Interview Questions — Chinese & English Summaries

《外企英文面试通关指南》四要素压缩版（每题中文/英文各 1 段）

说明：每道题均基于原文中的“相似问法、提问动机、答题思路、注意事项”压缩生成，适合做题库摘要、网页文案或面试复习提纲。

个人背景与自我认知

自我认知

Q1、Tell me about yourself. （跟我介绍一下你自己）

中文总结：这类题通常会换一种说法，围绕“跟我介绍一下你自己”这一主题追问你的经历、判断和表达。面试官主要看你的岗位匹配度、沟通表达、逻辑与结构化表达。回答时建议先简洁开场，再交代关键背景，突出相关经历与成果，说明与岗位的匹配，重点呈现量化证据、具体案例、岗位相关性；同时避免讲成流水账、空泛表述、跑题。

English Summary: This question may also appear as “Could you briefly introduce yourself ? / Let's start with you introducing yourself”. It is mainly used to assess role fit, communication, structured thinking. A strong answer should open briefly and professionally, cover your background selectively, highlight relevant achievements, link your experience to the role, with emphasis on specific evidence or metrics, one concrete example, clear relevance to the role. Keep the response concise and evidence-based, and do not turn it into a long timeline, avoid vague claims, do not drift off topic.

Q2、How would you describe yourself in 3 words? （你会用哪三个词来描述你自己？）

中文总结：这类题通常会换一种说法，围绕“你会用哪三个词来描述你自己”这一主题追问你的经历、判断和表达。面试官主要看你的逻辑与结构化表达。回答时建议采用“特质+解释+实例”的结构作答，重点呈现具体案例；同时避免空泛表述、表达失焦、把短板包装成优点。

English Summary: This question may also appear as “What three words would best describe your personality? / If you had to use three words to summarize yourself, what would they be?”. It is mainly used to assess structured thinking. A strong answer should use a “trait + explanation + example” structure, with emphasis on one concrete example. Keep the response concise and evidence-based, and avoid vague claims, keep it specific and well framed, do not dress up a weakness as a fake strength.

English Summary: This question may also appear as “What are the strengths that you bring to this position? / What’s your greatest strength?”. It is mainly used to assess role fit, communication, judgment. A strong answer should support them with examples, with emphasis on how you communicated. Keep the response concise and evidence-based, and keep it specific and well framed, avoid absolute wording.

Q4、What’s your biggest weakness?（你最大的缺点是什么？）

中文总结：这类题通常会换一种说法，围绕“你最大的缺点是什么”这一主题追问你的经历、判断和表达。面试官主要看你的岗位匹配度、沟通表达。回答时建议明确一个真实短板，说明改进动作，展示改善结果，重点呈现具体案例、成长与反思、结果与影响；同时避免暴露核心岗位短板、表达失焦、把短板包装成优点。

English Summary: This question may also appear as “Are there any aspects of your work or personal skills that you feel need development? / What do you consider to be your main areas for improvement?”. It is mainly used to assess role fit, communication, judgment. A strong answer should state one real development area, show what you did to improve, prove progress with results, with emphasis on one concrete example, growth and reflection, the outcome. Keep the response concise and evidence-based, and do not name a weakness that breaks role fit, keep it specific and well framed, do not dress up a weakness as a fake strength.

中文总结：这类题通常会换一种说法，围绕“你如何衡量自己工作的成功”这一主题追问你的经历、判断和表达。面试官主要看你的团队协作、价值观匹配、文化适应。回答时建议按“结论—例子—结果”的逻辑作答，重点呈现成长与反思、结果与影响；同时避免表达失焦、绝对化措辞。

English Summary: This question may also appear as “How do you define success in your professional role? / What criteria do you use to evaluate your work success?”. It is mainly used to assess teamwork, values alignment, cultural adaptability. A strong answer should give a structured, evidence-based response, with emphasis on growth and reflection, the outcome. Keep the response concise and evidence-based, and keep it specific and well framed, avoid absolute wording.
